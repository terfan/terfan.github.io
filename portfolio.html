UpSpire, webapp created for Dropbox Summer Hack Day 2015.

Typography Spring 2015, book cover project.

The assignment was to create a (purely typographic) design for a book cover for the short story The Necklace by Guy de Maupassant. Since a key player in the story is (spoiler alert!) an expensive diamond necklace that turns out to be fake, I decided to use Bayer, a Bauhaus play on French Didone fonts like Bodoni, and tuck it in amongst lettering in Bodoni, to hint at the twist ending of the story.
